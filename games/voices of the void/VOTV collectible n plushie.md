## argemia plushie
- main plushie
	- [ ] _red_ - [ X:625 / Y:-128 ], near Uniform, inside a crater
	- [ ] _green_ - [ X:239.2 / Y:828.6 ], on top of the mountain outside of the fence
	- [ ] _blue_ - [ X:-309 / Y:394 ], in the river near the Security Booth between the first two bridges when walking towards base
	- [ ] _cyan_ - Inside the base emergency shower after detonating _12 shrimp packs_ with an explosion
	- [ ] _magenta_ - [ X:634 / Y:44 ], near Uniform after The _Lifecrystal signal_ has been received
	- [ ] _yellow_ - On top of the basement grate after placing _5 shrimps packs_ at all _four corner of the fence-in including the corner within the forbidden zone_, turning away, and looking back
* nuclear plushie
	- [ ] _blue_ - [ X:872.25 / Y:-793.0 ], floating in the air on a small invisible cube, approximately the same height as the top of the wind turbines. require a method of flying or climbing
	- [ ] _cyan_ - [ X:-634.14 / Y:181.37 ] near Transformer 2, _buried_ in the ground on top a hill
	- [ ] _magenta_ - [ X:35.23 / Y:-37.24 ], invisible, in the sky on the same level as radio tower's railings. require an object to knock it down (drone is reccomended)
- ritual
	- [ ] _black_ - require _7 valid skull, all the normal argemia plushie and a hook_. place the 7 skulls at the center of _Stonehenge_ and remove everything that is not a skull from the epicenter. the player must hook themselves to the ground to not get sucked in while the _ritual_ beign. ==note: the ritual will delete all the normal argemia plushie be sure to make separate safe==
	- [ ] _white_ - require _all the normal argemia plushie_. each of the 6 Argemia plushies must be placed _5 meters apart from each other in a hexagon pattern_. while this ritual can be performed anywhere, the concrete foundation of the Radio Tower is the perfect size for pulling it off more easily. ==note: the ritual will delete all the normal argemia plushie be sure to make separate safe==
	- [ ] _gold_ - Stare at a _gold ingot_ for one real-life hour uninterrupted

- [ ] _keljoy plush_ - included in the player luggage upon creating and playing a new save for the forst time
- [ ] _kel doll_ - require _loyal_ ariral reputation by the day 25. the _Brownie trail event_ will instruct the player to go to ariral treehouse and retrieve it
- [ ] _antibreather plush_ - Inside the bigger nest in the _cave at exactly 3:33_. ==the player need to enter the cave before 3:33==
- [ ] _charborg plush/charcoal fish_ - obtain by fishing. will grant the _charcoal fish achivement_ and unlock the _charbog hat_ in the store
- [ ] _llama plushie_ - require funny setting enable. put _llama.saatana_ command on the console to summon the plushie. check [[funny console command and debug]] for more info
- [ ] _thiccfus Plush_ - Thiccfus plush is dropped upon killing Rufus. check [[funny console command and debug]] on how to summon rufus
